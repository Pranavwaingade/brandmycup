# ☕ BrandMyCup

Build Your Brand, One Cup at a Time.

BrandMyCup is a modern MERN Stack e-commerce platform that enables businesses to design, customize, and order premium-quality paper cups with their own branding. The platform provides an intuitive customization experience, secure ordering, and a powerful admin panel for managing products and customer orders.

---

## 🚀 Project Overview

BrandMyCup is designed for cafés, restaurants, tea stalls, juice centers, event organizers, startups, and small businesses that want custom-branded paper cups for their business.

Customers can browse products, customize paper cups, upload their brand logo, choose cup specifications, and place bulk orders online.
